oldWeather3 - U.S. Arctic logbooks¶

The Logbook of USGS Pioneer, as transcribed by oldWeather. (Video page).

oldWeather3, also known as “oldWeather Arctic”, or “oldWeather classic”, was a follow-on project to the original oldWeather. It took essentially the same approach, but used a different set of original documents - U.S. Government ships logbooks in the US ?National Archives, concentrating on voyages to the Arctic. (Sample image).

The project included 200,000 images, from only 18 different ships.
